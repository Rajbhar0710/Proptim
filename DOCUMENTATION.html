Common Questions

Quick answers to things people often ask.

What kind of properties does PROPTIM deal in?

Only commercial properties — offices, retail shops, restaurant spaces, and warehouses. No residential properties.

Which cities does PROPTIM cover?

Mainly Chhattisgarh (Raipur, Durg-Bhilai, Korba, Bilaspur) and Madhya Pradesh (Bhopal, Indore, Jabalpur). Raipur is the headquarters.

Is the consultation free?

Yes, the first consultation is free. PROPTIM understands your requirements and then explains how they can help. Any fees are discussed openly upfront.

I own a property. How do I tell PROPTIM about it?

Click "Propose Property" on the website, fill the form with your property details and photos, and submit. The team will review it and contact you if it matches a client's need.

What happens after I submit my property?

Your details are saved securely, a PDF report is auto-generated, and the PROPTIM team is notified instantly. If your property fits an active client requirement, they will call you.

Is my information kept confidential?

Yes. Client confidentiality is one of PROPTIM's core values. Your property details and personal information are never shared without your permission.

How is PROPTIM different from a regular broker?

Every deal is handled by the founder personally — not passed to a junior. Recommendations come with data and analysis. The focus is on finding what actually fits your need, not just showing spaces.

Does the website work on my phone?

Yes. The website works perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers. All forms and buttons are easy to tap on a small screen.

Can I call instead of filling a form?

Absolutely. Call 9165477999 or 9755303306 anytime. The "Call Now" button on the website connects you directly.
